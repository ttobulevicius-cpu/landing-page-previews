Our Practice Areas

We focus on specific areas of law to provide the highest quality representation for our clients.

We represent consumers who have purchased defective vehicles. If your car has a substantial defect that the manufacturer can't fix after a reasonable number of attempts, call us.

Breach of Warranty

When a manufacturer fails to uphold the promises made in their warranty, you have rights. We fight to enforce those warranties under state and federal laws.
