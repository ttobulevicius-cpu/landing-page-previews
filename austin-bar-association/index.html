Advancing the Practice of Independent Law

The Solo/Small Firm Section of the Austin Bar Association is dedicated to the unique needs of attorneys in solo practice or small firms.

We provide a robust platform for networking, education, and resource sharing, ensuring that independent practitioners have the same competitive edge as large firms. Our community is built on mutual support and professional growth.
