Real Change. Hard Truths.

The Institute for Inclusion in the Legal Profession (IILP) is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating a more diverse and inclusive legal profession. We believe that for diversity and inclusion to be achieved, we must rely on data, research, and open dialogue.

Under the leadership of our COO, we strive to bridge the gap between good intentions and measurable results.

"Inclusion needs you. The future of our profession depends on our ability to embrace diversity in all its forms."

Linda Chanow Chief Operating Officer
