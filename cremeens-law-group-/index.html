Practice Areas

Lemon Law Claims

If your new or leased vehicle has a significant defect that the dealer cannot fix, you may be entitled to a refund or replacement under Florida law.

Breach of Warranty

Federal protection under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act covering defective consumer products, ensuring manufacturers honor their warranties.

Dealer Fraud

Protecting consumers against deceptive trade practices, undisclosed accident damage, and odometer rollbacks by dealerships.
